I completed an internship at NAVER LABS as a Human-Robot Interaction designer. I worked with UX designers, product designers, robotics engineers, and HRI researchers. I participated in ‘project 1784’ to construct the world’s first robot-friendly building. The project expects that a robot platform can make robots autonomously run on a road, going beyond working inside the building by expanding robots’ service areas from the inside of the building to the outside space. In particular, the platform allows a robot to function for users’ purposes, such as carrying out robotic delivery services and working at automatic shops.
