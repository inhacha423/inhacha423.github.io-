Design for a Better Tomorrow

Majoring in Human-Computer Interaction & Design with a background in philosophy

Welcome! I am currently a masters student in the department of Industrial Design at KAIST and
work as a research assistant in the Creative Interaction Design Lab lead by Prof. Youn-Kyung Lim.

My research interests include exploring the use of technological artifacts to encourage for
individuals to empower self-determination, and support communities to connect with people.
Furthermore, I am also interested in sparking a discussion about the socio-cultural and ethical
implications of emerging technologies, such as AI recommendation systems or robots.
Throughout the research processes, I employ co-design and participatory design methods to
engage users in the design practice.
